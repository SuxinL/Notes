# General Engineering Safety

We need to protect both people and devices.

- people
    - operators
        - no long hair
        - wearings
            - clothing
                - no loose clothes
                - sleeves fastened
                - neckties ans scarfs inside 
            - no metal attachments
                - watches
                - jewelries
            - safety glasses
        - no risky operations
            - lifting too heavy objects
    - others
        - at a safe place
        - no risky operations 
- devices 
    - at a safe place
        - not in the way
        - always from others
    - cleanup
        - complete reinstallation before using
        - tools at original places

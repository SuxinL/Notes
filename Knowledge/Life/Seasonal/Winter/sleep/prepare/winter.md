# Sleep in Winter

- CASE
    - QUILT_NOT_ENOUGH: I feel cold if only using a quilt.
    - HEAD: A quilt can not cover my head well. If I expose my head out I can not fall into sleep. If I cover my head with a corner of the same quilt I need to keep still to avoid my feet being exposed. 
    - SOCKS_NEEDED: My feet feel cold if not wearing socks.
- THOUGHT
    - CONDITION
        1. WINTER: when the weather is cold.
    - PURPOSE
        - EFFICIENCY
            - COMFORT
                - BREATHE: I can breathe freely.
                - MOVE: My body can adjust to a comfortable sleeping posture. 
        - SAFETY
            - WARMTH
                - BODY
                - FEET
            - FEELING
                - HEAD: I need to cover my head to get a feeling of safety.
    - PRINCIPLE
        - for BREATHE
            - LIE_FACE_UP: to avoid blocking the air flow
        - for BREATHE v.s HEAD
            - BALANCE
                - COVER_UPPER_HEAD: only cover my upper head, and expose my mouth out.
        - for BODY
            - FRONT
                - COMFORTER
                    - CONDUCTION
                        - THICK
                    - CONVECTION
                        - FOLD_EDGES
            - BACK
                - WOOL_BED_SHEET
        - for FEET
            - SOCKS
        - for MOVE
            - LARGE_COVER
        - for BODY v.s COVER_UPPER_HEAD
            - DIVIDE&CONQUER
                - SEPARATE_COVERS: one for body, another for head 
    - PROCEDURE
        - seasonal preparation

            2. mind

            3. neck

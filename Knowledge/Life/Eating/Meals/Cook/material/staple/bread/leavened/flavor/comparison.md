# sourdough v.s rye v.s challah

- comparison
    | Aspect | sourdough | rye | challah |
    | --- | --- | --- | --- |
    | special components | naturally occurring microbes as leavening agents | rye flour | sugar, egg, oil |
    | flavor | mildly sour | earthy | sweet |
    | use case | others | with sharp dishes like cured meats | snack |
- procedure
    - S: if to pair with sharp dishes
        - rye
    - S: else if to eat as snack
        - challah
    - O: otherwise
        - sourdough

# chemical v.s yeast v.s sourdough

- comparison
    | Aspect | chemical | yeast | sourdough |
    | --- | --- | --- | --- |
    | mechanism | use gas-producing chemicals like baking soda | manufactured yeasts ferment sugar to produce CO2 | use naturally occurring microbes |
    | M | MIDDLE | HIGH | MIDDLE |
    | T | SHORT | MIDDLE | LONG |
    | L | LOW | MIDDLE | HIGH |
    | effect | good | good | good |
    | flavor | LOW | MIDDLE | HIGH |
    | use case | fast | others | special flavor |
- procedure
    - T: if I want fast making
        - chemical
    - F: else if I want a special flavor
        - sourdough
    - O: otherwise
        - yeast

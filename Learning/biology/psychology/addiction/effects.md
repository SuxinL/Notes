# Addiction Effects

There are some general effects of addictions.
- direct: wasting a lot of time.
- aftermath
    - task delayed 
        - sleep deficit
        - work not done
    - poor brain performance
        - low cognitive ability 
        - low concentration
    - negative emotions
        - upset
        - depression
        - anxiety

# BlockSite

## What

A productivity app to block unwanted sites and apps.

## Purposes

- efficiency
    - avoid distractions
- security
    - avoid adult contents

## When

- working time to improve productivity
- leisure time to avoid sleeping too late

## Where

- apps
    - android
    - ios
    - windows
- browser extensions
